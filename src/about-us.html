Our Company

Leading IT Solutions Provider Since 2009

Smartcomp Solutions, Inc. has established itself as one of the leading players in the market for IT-based solutions geared towards the branch operations of banks, retail establishments, and the PC industry. Registered at SEC on March 24, 2009, our office is located at RM 611 Royal Plaza Twin Tower 648 Remedios St. cor. Ma. Orosa St. Malate, Manila.

We started as Sycom System and Services, Inc. on March 1, 2001, with SM Department Store Branches as our first client, providing resident engineers for Point of Sales (POS) systems across all SM Branches. In 2004, we expanded our services to include Banking Systems and Solutions, including ATMs, Cash Handling Systems, and Clearing Systems.

In March 2009, we merged with Retail Asia, a company with 4 years of experience in the retail industry, and established Smartcomp Solutions Inc. to cater to the growing needs for technical support in the retail, banking, and PC industries. Today, we have 125 Customer Service Engineers deployed across the country, ensuring reliable and efficient service nationwide.

Our Services

Comprehensive IT Solutions

We offer a wide range of IT services tailored to meet your business needs

Technical Support

Nationwide IT support for POS systems, ATMs, kiosks, and other IT equipment. Our team of 125 Customer Service Engineers provides prompt and efficient service across the country.

Banking & Retail Solutions

Installation, configuration, testing, and maintenance of ATMs and POS systems. We provide end-to-end services from site survey to permit processing and ongoing support.

Network Infrastructure

Structured cabling installation and testing, router and switch setup, backbone connection checking, and configuration of access points for reliable network connectivity.

Preventive Maintenance

Regular maintenance services for POS systems, PCs, servers, and other IT equipment to ensure optimal performance and prevent costly downtime.

Resident Engineers

Logistics & Deployment

Door-to-door delivery of IT equipment and ATMs nationwide, warehousing services, and IMACD (Install, Move, Add, Change, Deploy) services for efficient deployment.
